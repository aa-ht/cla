Services

Chits

Chit is an economic activity helps an individual to meet his/her financial needs with a desired structure of payments & terms. On the other hand it also makes a person to attain a financial discipline by saving money for his future endeavors with regular contribution in a chit. Aishwarya Lakshmi chits supports an customer/ Subscriber to resolve his financial barriers also to meet his needs & wants.

Shares Small Savings

Small savings scheme is derived of formed to customers with little earnings who cannot afford to engage in chits. Scheme designed with a monthly installment of Rs.1,000 payment from a subscriber for a period of 9 months. For further information please reach us on phone or e-mail.

MITHRA

MITHRA provides solution to a customer/Subscriber to face unexpected small commitments in his daily routine. This service is formulated with monthly payment of small amounts. Funds are granted to overcome his financial obstacles with simple & hassle free payments. For further information related to the scheme please reach us on phone or e-mail .
