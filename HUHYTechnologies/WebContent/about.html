About HUHY Technologies

Our custom developed solutions have demonstrated higher value, lower cost of ownership and consistent performance in the last 7 years. Not to mention, we have managed to maintain 100% client retention rate for our custom developed applications.

Start-ups, ISVs, Enterprises alike have leveraged our custom software application development services to build reliable, scalable and low cost solutions based on their unique process or design requirements. We owe our success to various flexible engagement models, together with expertise in technology components, open source softwares, software tools and multi-platform environments.

Custom Software Development Services Include:

  • Custom Software Development and Maintenance
  • Platform modernization and Legacy Application Migration
  • Custom Application Consulting
  • Internationalization and localization of Web applications
  • Application support and maintenance

HUHY Technologies offers half and full-day hands-on workshops for technology enthusiast who want to explore JAVA, Python, PHP, Big Data, Android, Oracle, MySQL, Unix and related Technologies. We create a welcoming environment for those who are hesitant around technology and we emphasize skills that can be put into practice right away. At HUHY, we have a good time with productive workshops.

We strive to be inclusive and accessible. We keep our prices as low as we can and that means we get a great balance of attendees. Trainings are held during the day, evenings, and weekends so that people can attend during the comfortable hours. We rely heavily on the digital skills. Our most successful marketing comes through e-mail newsletters and social media and we have strong word-of-mouth marketing for our sessions.

We're also proud to say that repeat attendance is high -- we often find that someone will take one session at HUHY, and then want to take more, and bring their friends and colleagues.

Key element to HUHY's step to success is our commitment to responding to our attendee's needs. We ask for feedback after every workshop, and we make deliberate changes to our curriculum based on those responses. We also take suggestions for workshops topics so we stay relevant to the needs of the changing digital landscape. If there's enough interest in a topic, we find a great instructor and add it to our roster.

The other key element to our success lies in providing a great experience for our attendees. We really try to make our attendees feel comfortable and welcome. We find that when people are in a relaxed and fun environment, it becomes easier for them to understand new concepts. Then when we see someone really 'get it' for the first time, that's when we know we've hit the mark. We like to say that HUHY Technologies is for anyone curious to learn more about technology.
